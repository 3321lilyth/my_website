My Family

My name is Lijia Yang (楊立嘉), and "Jia(嘉)" represents goodness, beauty, auspiciousness, and happiness. My mother named me with the intention of being born in Chiayi, and hoping for a happy and prosperous life. My father is a telecommunications engineer and my mother was once a Ph.D. in Chinese. They strive to manage a warm and happy family, hoping that my brother and I can both achieve a simple and resilient character, and they encourage us to explore and learn actively. My brother is a graduate student in Mechanical Engineering at NTU, we share many common interests, such as watching anime and singing Japanese songs.
